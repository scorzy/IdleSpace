Automators

Automators are unlocked by automator level Automators "Automator Level" is prestige level * (1 + 0.3 * ascension level) and never decrease.
Automators can automate some aspect of the game, for example auto search for new enemy.

Automators Stats
  • Active: On Turn Automator on or off
  • Active: When not have Robot Factory Turn Automator off if you have at least one Drone Factory
  • IntervalMinimum interval, unlock lower interval with prestige level. You should put higher interval on low priority automators to avoid Priority inversion
  • PriorityHigher number equal higher priority
  • Res %Percent of resource to use. With 100% automators will use 100% of available resource
